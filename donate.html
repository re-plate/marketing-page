Donate Food with Replate

Are you the owner, manager, or employee of a restaurant, bakery, market, or anywhere else that sells and ends up throwing away food? Looking for a way to reduce that waste and help both people and the planet? Make an easy and tax-deductable donation with replate by following these easy steps.

Register

Fill out our simple form with your contact information. We use this to match you with volunteers in your area. Replate never shares your information with third parties

Request a Pickup

Log on to our user friendly platform and request a pickup. Your request will instantly notify volunteers in your area that you have food available to donate, and allow them to view details and accept the pickup.

Meet Your Courier

Once a courier has accepted your request, they will come and pick up the donation and deliver it to a local place in need, such as a food bank, soup kitchen, or shelter. That's it!
