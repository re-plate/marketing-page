Volunteer to be a Courier

Replate makes it easy for you to get involved and help reduce food waste and stop hunger in your community. In just three easy steps, you can make a big difference for people in need.

Register

Fill out our simple form with your contact information. We use this to match you with restaurants and other businesses in your area. Replate never shares your information with third parties.

Accept a Pickup

Once you've signed up, use our intuitive platform to find a business near you that has food they'd like to donate. Our platform allows you to work with business owners or managers to coordinate a convenient time for the both of you.

Deliver to a Local Charity

Replate works with charities across the country that assist food insecure individuals, including food banks, soup kitchens, and homeless shelters. Once you've picked up the donation, simply deliver it to the specified charity.
